The problem

Career guidance is scattered across four different tabs.

One site for courses. Another for resume reviews. A third for interview prep. A fourth for project ideas — and none of them remember you between visits.

Fragmented

No single source of truth

Skill gaps, courses, and interview prep live on unrelated platforms with no shared context.

Stateless

Chatbots forget you

One-time answers with no memory of prior progress, so guidance never compounds.

Generic

One-size-fits-all advice

Recommendations rarely adapt to a specific resume, goal, or pace of learning.

Uncoordinated

No orchestration

Resume review, roadmap, and interview prep need to talk to each other — most tools don't let them.

One planner. Six specialists. Zero forgotten context.

How it works

Every session flies the same checklist.

STEP 1

Upload your resume

The Resume Analysis Agent extracts your current skills and flags concrete improvements — formatting, missing sections, weak bullet points.

STEP 2

Set your target role

The Goal Mapping and Skill Analysis Agents compute exactly what's missing between where you are and where you're headed.

STEP 3

Get your flight plan

The Roadmap and Recommendation Agents generate a phased weekly plan with courses and portfolio projects for each gap.

STEP 4

Track progress over time

The Memory & Progress Agent remembers every session, so your roadmap adapts instead of resetting each time you come back.
